Nagaon Beach

Located just a short drive away, Nagaon Beach is known for its clean shores and calm waters, making it a perfect spot for swimming, beach sports, and relaxation amidst coconut trees.

Alibaug Beach

Famous for its stunning sunsets and the Alibaug Fort visible during low tide, Alibaug Beach is ideal for long walks along the shore and enjoying local seafood delicacies.

Kolaba Fort

A historic sea fort located near Alibaug Beach, Kolaba Fort offers visitors a glimpse into the Maratha Empire’s past. Explore its old structures and enjoy panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

Varsoli Beach

A quieter, more secluded beach compared to others in the area, Varsoli Beach is perfect for those looking to unwind and enjoy peaceful moments by the sea, surrounded by beautiful natural scenery.

Karmarkar Museum

A unique museum showcasing the works of renowned sculptor Nanasaheb Karmarkar. This small but intriguing space displays a range of exquisite sculptures that art lovers will appreciate.

Revdanda Beach

Just a short distance from Alibaug, Revdanda Beach is famous for its scenic beauty and tranquility. Visitors can explore the ruins of an ancient Portuguese fort and enjoy stunning views of the coastline.

Kihim Beach

Kihim Beach is renowned for its golden sands and lush greenery. It's an excellent destination for nature lovers, offering opportunities for bird watching and leisurely walks along the shore.

Awas Beach

A hidden gem, Awas Beach is less crowded and provides a serene environment perfect for unwinding. Visitors can enjoy water activities or simply relax while enjoying the picturesque views.

Sasawane Beach

Sasawane Beach is known for its clean, quiet surroundings and beautiful landscapes. It’s an ideal spot for families and couples looking for a peaceful day at the beach, away from the hustle and bustle.

Mandwa Beach

Mandwa Beach is known for its pristine sand and serene atmosphere, making it a popular spot for picnics and water sports. It's also a great place to enjoy a relaxing day away from the crowds.
